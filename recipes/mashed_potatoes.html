Mashed Potatoes

Description

So many people, when asked for their favorite vegetable, immediately say "potatoes," and for good reason. This creamy mashed potatoes recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds! Follow along with the recipe below to make a great meal that you can enjoy any day. Get your ingredients ready and let's get to cooking!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Peel and boil the potatoes
  2. Put all the boiled potatoes in a bowl
  3. Add butter, salt, and a dollop of sour cream
  4. Mash until the ingredients are all mixed together
