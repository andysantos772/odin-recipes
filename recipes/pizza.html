Pizza

Description

This delicious cheese pizza recipe unites fresh mozzarella and tasty tomatoes to make something amazing! There's space for improvisation with toppings but the recipe focuses on a good foundation, so we'll make sure to get that right before anything else. This is a quick recipe you can follow whenever you're craving a pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Shape the dough into a circle
  2. Blend the tomatoes into a sauce and add salt
  3. Shred the mozzarella cheese
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough
  5. Add the shredded cheese on top of the sauce
  6. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes at 400 degrees Fahrenheit
